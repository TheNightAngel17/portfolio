## Overview
After the C-Suite at MCHS decided to go with a packaged EHR instead of our home-grown MCIS Clinicals, I was moved to work on supporting Cattails Dental, our Fork of the open-sourced Open Dental, with changes specific to our practice, focusing on integrating with many different items and automated billing.
